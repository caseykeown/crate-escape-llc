Who We Are

A one-stop shop
built for your dog.

Crate Escape was built from the ground up in Grayson County to give local dogs a real alternative to sitting in a cage at the vet. Our facility is staffed around the clock, and every boarding dog joins our daycare pack during the day — playing, swimming, and socializing the way dogs are meant to.

From daycare and overnight boarding to professional grooming, mobile grooming, and veterinary services, everything your pet needs is here at 60 Bark Ave.
